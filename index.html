About

Not Your Usual Drawing Session

Wyrd Life Drawing turns the traditional figure session into an immersive, otherworldly night. Each event is a themed world — medieval, fantastical, post-apocalyptic — built with cinematic lighting, elaborate costumes and atmospheric sound.

Our models arrive with their own personas and costumes, and creative direction rotates between our founders — so every night is genuinely one of a kind. All skill levels welcome.
