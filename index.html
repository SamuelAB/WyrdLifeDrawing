About

Wyrd Life Drawing transforms the traditional life drawing session into an immersive, otherworldly experience. Each event is a unique themed night — from medieval fantasies to enchanted forest spirits — complete with cinematic lighting, fantastical costumes, and atmospheric music.

We alternate creative direction between our two founders, bringing fresh perspectives and new models to every session. Our models arrive with their own personas and elaborate costumes, making each event a truly one-of-a-kind artistic experience.

Whether you're a seasoned artist or picking up a pencil for the first time, Wyrd Life Drawing welcomes all skill levels. Come draw, be inspired, and lose yourself in the atmosphere.
